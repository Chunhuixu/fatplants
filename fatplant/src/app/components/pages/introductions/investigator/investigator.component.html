Dr. Doug Allen Dr. Allen is a USDA-ARS Research Scientist and Principal Investigator at the Donald Danforth Plant Science Center. His research focuses on quantitative descriptions of central carbon metabolism in plants that includes photosynthetic carbon assimilation and partitioning into important storage reserves such as protein and oil.
